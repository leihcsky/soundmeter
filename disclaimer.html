Measurement Accuracy

Sound Meter provides estimated measurements that depend on multiple factors:

  • Device Microphone: Consumer device microphones are not calibrated for professional sound measurement
  • Browser Implementation: Different browsers may implement the Web Audio API differently
  • Environmental Factors: Background noise, microphone positioning, and acoustic environment affect readings
  • Device Settings: Volume settings, audio processing, and hardware quality impact accuracy
  • Calibration: The tool is not calibrated to professional sound level meter standards

Measurement variations of ±10 dB or more are possible. Do not rely on these measurements for critical decisions.
