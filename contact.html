Common Technical Issues

Microphone Not Working?

  • Check browser permissions in settings
  • Ensure microphone is not used by another application
  • Try a different browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)
  • Check if your device microphone is functioning properly

Inaccurate Readings?

  • Remember: this is an educational tool, not professional equipment
  • Accuracy depends on your device's microphone quality
  • Try positioning your device differently
  • Check if background apps are processing audio

Privacy Concerns?

  • We never record or transmit audio
  • All processing happens locally in your browser
  • Read our Privacy Policy for details
