This page lists major changes to our tools, user-facing guidance, and accessibility improvements. Minor copy edits and small styling tweaks may not always be logged.

2026-04-26

Navigation and hearing-age rollout updates
  • Launched the new Hearing Age Test experience with volume calibration, continuous high-frequency sweep, and inline shareable result flow.
  • Updated the global header to use a Hearing dropdown (Online hearing test + Hearing age test) across key tools and content pages.
  • Aligned mobile navigation with a nested Hearing submenu and improved consistency across homepage, hearing tools, and blog pages.
  • Refreshed changelog and footer navigation consistency to match the main homepage information architecture.

2026-03-30

Localization differentiation updates
  • Rewrote multi-language guidance to use original phrasing with reference links (less templated “According to …” copy).

  • Customized the three sample report captions per locale (titles, interpretation notes, and measurement tips) to reflect more realistic local scenarios.
